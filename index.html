Catholic Short Prayers

★★★★★ App Store Rating

Helping you to pray at all times with these short prayers ready at hand. "Pray without ceasing. In all circumstances give thanks, for this is the will of God for you in Christ Jesus." - 1 Thessalonians 5:17-18 - There is daily reminder feature for each prayer to help you pray more consistently. Or use the location based reminder feature to get reminder to pray when you reach your home, office, etc. You can shake the device to get to random short prayer. Share your favorite short prayers through Twitter, Facebook, email and many more through the app. Please pray for holy souls in Purgatory and also for me as I continue to develop Catholic apps.
